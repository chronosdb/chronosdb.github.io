Ready to take control over your time series.

ChronosDB is a time series database for the low frequency business, economic, and financial time series, which makes collaboration, storage, and management of time series simple.

Request early access now

Organize

End the chaos of messy folders with multiple revisions of the same files. ChronosDB lets you organize your time series in collections and spaces. Access is controlled down to the individual time series.

Time Travel

It is not only GDP and unemployment that are revised. In any organization, the time series produced by analysts are changing on a daily basis due to errors, restatements, and restructuring. ChronosDB helps you keep track of historical changes.

Collaboration

ChronosDB allows individuals and teams to collaborate regardless of their technical proficiency. WebUI, Python/R clients, and Excel plugin let the data flow seamlessly across your teams.

Data Quality

Managing and exchanging time series data in flat files or spreadsheets results in bad data, lower productivity, and revenue loss. Harvard Business Review reports that analysts spend 50 percent of their time searching for data, correcting errors and seeking out confirmatory sources for data they don’t trust. ChronosDB is a storage solution, which will make analysts more productive, restore confidence in the organization’s data; and improve the bottom line.

Features

Fully versioned

Keep track of the data changes. Historical data is accessible by a revision date or a tag.

Time index

The time index is a first-class citizen. ChronosDB supports daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual frequencies out of the box.

Metadata

Keep units, descriptions, and documentation for all your time series with your data.

Annotations

Numbers don’t capture the entire story. Preserve important qualitative information by annotating the series and individual observations.

Reproducibility

Save the model parameters with your model output; just in case you are asked to reproduce the results.

Hierarchical data

National accounts or ledger data are supported out of the box.

Panel data

Support for cross-section data for each entity, whether it is a household, corporation, or division in your company.

Inclusiveness

Everyone regardless of their technical prowess can effectively share and collaborate.

Granular access control

Grant access to working spaces, collections, and individual time series
