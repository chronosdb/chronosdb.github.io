Organizing Timeseries (Spaces and Collections)

#Organizing Timeseries (Spaces and Collections)

Individual time series are organized in collections, which are assigned to spaces. You have permissions for

#Space

A space is a place where a user stores collections.

#Collection

A collection is at the center of ChronosDB. It is a place to store time series of various types and frequencies. Within each collection time series are versioned with vintages created for every data write. Annotations are confined within a collection.

A collection is independent from the other collections; it can be archived and removed from the database without affecting other collections.

Permissions are also managed on the collection level.
