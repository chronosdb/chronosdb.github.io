Introduction

ChronosDB is a time-series database for the business, economic, and financial time series. tshub is a SaaS time-series platform running on ChronosDB and providing storage, management, and collaboration tools for time-series as well as business intelligence, visualization, and time-series analytics.

Key features of ChronosDB are:

  • fully-versioned.
  • support for various time indices (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and annual).
  • units, description, methodology are stored with the series.
  • preserve contextual information about your data with annotation of time series, individual observations, or periods.
